About Us

Fun and Friendly LeetCode Competition!

Preparing for technical interviews can be stressful and discouraging, especially when you're trying to stay consistent on your own. We wanted to make the process more supportive, social, and enjoyable.

Our platform helps friend groups stay motivated by turning LeetCode practice into a friendly competition. You can track progress together, encourage each other, and build positive habits without feeling isolated.

Whether you're preparing for internship recruiting or full-time interviews, our goal is to make the experience more manageable and more fun!

Created by Sophie Lin, Ashley Yang, Nessa Tong, and Jessica Dai.
